Discover The Beautiful World

Kashmir

Heaven on Earth Kashmir is one of the most beautiful travel destinations to visit in North India. Nowhere in India you will behold the scenic landscapes, icy glaciers, pristine lakes and lofty mountains as beautiful as Kashmir. Serenity and Tranquility redefines itself from the ambiance of Kashmir. Be it summer, Winter or Monsoon, every season has its own charm in Kashmir. The numerous wonders of nature, culture, cuisines and rich history of Kashmir will elevate your travel experience in Incredible India.

Manali

Manali, that offers unique historical and cultural riches together has hosted many different civilizations with its geography spread over two continents. This unique city which is admired by its charming nature and the attractive atmosphere is also the symbol of dynamic and modern city life. Istanbul has a multicultural texture and a lively atmosphere that melts the past and future in a single pot which provides a sense of universal history at every step.

Spiti

Spiti is a remote and picturesque region located in the northern part of India, primarily in the state of Himachal Pradesh. It is known for its stunning landscapes, high mountains, and unique cultural heritage. .

Shimla

Shimla, the capital city of the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, is a popular hill station and tourist destination known for its scenic beauty, pleasant climate, and rich colonial history.

LEH

Leh is the largest town and the administrative capital of the Ladakh region in the northernmost part of the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, high-altitude landscapes, and rich cultural heritage.

Rishikesh

Rishikesh is a popular town in the northern Indian state of Uttarakhand, located in the foothills of the Himalayas along the banks of the sacred Ganges River. It is known for its spiritual significance, natural beauty, and its status as a hub for yoga, meditation, and adventure activities.

Chopta

Chopta is a picturesque and serene hill station and trekking destination located in the state of Uttarakhand in northern India. It is part of the Kedarnath Wildlife Sanctuary and offers stunning views of the Himalayan mountain range, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and trekkers.

Kasol

Kasol is a small village in the Parvati Valley, located in the Kullu district of the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It has gained popularity as a backpacker's paradise and is known for its scenic beauty, relaxed atmosphere, and proximity to stunning natural landscapes.
